Biography of Christophe Maé

Christophe Maé was born in a family of musicians in the charming village of Carpentras. At young age he learnt how to play the guitare, the violin and the drums. At sixteen Christophe becomes really sick. He has to keep in bed and so focusses on singing and playing his harmonica. Because of this he is now a complete artist with a repertoire that has soul and blues. Christophe has also proven to have some acting skills as he played in a very popular musical comedy 'Roi Soleil'.
