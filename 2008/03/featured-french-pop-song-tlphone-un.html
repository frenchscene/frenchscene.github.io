Featured French Pop Song :: Téléphone - Un autre Monde

So this is the idea: next to French Pop artists I will also try to regularly feature a French Pop song.

The first one EVER to be featured on FrenchScene.Net is a real French Pop classic : Téléphone - Un autre Monde ("an other world").




The song is pretty old (1984) but it has been covered a lot by various French artists.

As we have just discussed Emma Daumas, I thought I'd post a cover by her and Yannick Noah
