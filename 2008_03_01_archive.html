Featured French Pop Classic : France Gall - Ella, Elle L'a

Let's dive back in to French Pop history today with a French Pop classic of France Gall "Ella, Elle L'a" ("Ella, she's got it").

The song was actually made as a tribute to Ella Fitzgerald, an important jazz vocalist who won 13 Grammy Awards. The song was composed by France Gall's husband Michel Berger.



Quelque chose dans la voix (Something in her voice)
Qui parat nous dire "viens" (Seems to tell us "come")
Qui nous fait sentir trangement bien (Makes us feel good)
C'est comme toute l'histoire (It's like the entire history)
Du peuple noir (of the black people)
Qui se balance (that balances)

Featured French Pop Artist : Raphaël

Raphaël is today's featured artists, his intimate relation with music started at the age of 5, when he learned to play the piano. Later he would drop out of law school to focus on his career as a musician.

Raphaël Fast Facts

  • Raphaël was influenced by Jacques Brel, Noir Désir, Iggy Pop, Bowie, Ferré

  • His career got a kick start when he met Caroline Manset daughter of important composer Gérard Manset, she becomes his manager, together they composed songs for an album

  • Music label "EMI" was impressed, immediately they let Rahaël sign a contract

  • In 2000 he's happy to announce his first CD "Hôtel de l’Univers"

  • In 2002 one of his biggest dreams comes through, he gets to open the show of David Bowie

  • In 2003 his second CD "La Réalité" is seen as a confirmation of his talent, he composed somewhat all the songs by himself

  • With his third CD "Caravane" it becomes clear that Rahaël is one of the most creative artists of his generation

  • After a live album "Résistance à la Nuit" he starts composing again which results in his new CD (released in march 2008) "Je sais que la Terre est plate" ("I know the earth is flat")

Vanessa Paradis, yep that's Johnny Depp's Girl

Vanessa Paradis is probably one of the most popular French artists. In France she's known as a talented singer and actress, in the rest of the world people mainly know her for her relationship with actor Johnny Depp. She has been a French Pop icon for as long as people can remember and it looks like she's still in the game for years to come. Her 2007 CD "Divine Idylle" was a huge success, she recently received best female artist and best album award in the French Pop Music Awards.



Vanessa Paradis Bio (some "fast facts")
  • In 1986 Vanessa Paradis released her first single "Joe Le Taxi", the song was an instant hit in all European charts. She even made it to the popular BBC program "top of the pops", which was then quite extraordinary for a French artist.

  • In 1987 Vanessa presents her debute album "M & J"

  • In 1989 she becomes an actrice in her first movie "Noce Blanche", in 1990 she recieves the Cèsar (French movie award) for "most promising new talent"

  • In 1990 she meets Serge Gainsbourg (one of the most popular French singers in French music history) who will write the lyrics for her new CD "Variations sur le même t'aime". She will receive the Best Female Artist Award for this album.

  • In 1992 she will sing in English for the first time, in a collaboration with Lenny Kravitz she releases her third album "Vanessa Paradis"

  • In 1993 Vanessa toured for the first time giving more than 10 concerts

  • From 1994 on Vanessa focusses on her career as an actress, she will act in several movies next to great French actors like Gérard Depardieu.

  • In 2000 the patience of Vanessa's numerous die-hard fans is rewarded : the 17th of October her new album hits the markets, "Bliss" is a tribute to her husband Johnny Depp
    and their child. 300 000 copies of the album are sold.

  • Vanessa likes to take her time, when she does something she likes to do it right. Her fifth album comes out in 2007 and is well received by the audience. She's awarded with the Best French Female Artist and Best Album award!

Emma Daumas, talented French pop star

Today I'm going to talk to you about Emma Daumas, a young talented French pop star.



I know you're here for the music, so I will try to make the bio part short and just give you some "fast facts" about every artist.

  • Emma Daumas was borne in Avignon in 1982

  • Her real name is actually "Emmanuelle"

  • Her passion for music started at a very young age, with the help of her parents she recorderd a record when she was 12!

  • She plays the piano & the guitare

  • Emma became really popular when she ended as semi-finalist in the French second Star Academy

  • Emma is often called 'the French Avril Lavigne'

  • Her first big appearance took place in 2003 in Strasbourg where she performed "Call Me" (Blondie) before an audience of 8000 people!
Emma Daumas' Music : First Singles

Au jour le jour


In February 2003 Emma Daumas released her first single "Au jour le jour" ("from day to day") , a song she composed herself. The song is about "living in the now", the carpe-diem spirit. You should enjoy everyday, without looking at the past.

Carla Bruni, what is she really about?

Everybody's talking about the new wife of the French president Sarkozy. Yes you know her name : Carla Bruni. What you probably don't know is that she's also a popular French singer!!

Let's start with a pic to fresh up your mind



Yep that's Carla for you!

And now about her music. Her first cd namely 'Quelqu'Un M'A Dit ' ("someone told me") came out in 2002. I listened to most of the tracks and though it's not really my 'thing' I did kind of enjoy it. If you like whispery, acoustique, blues music this is def something for you. The lyrics are mostly about love.

The title song ''Quelqu'Un M'A Dit' (her most popular song)



In 2007 Carla released her second CD "No Promises"...it's in English...boooooh




So this was a short introduction into Carla's singing career, though I'm not really 'a fan' we should give her some credit, she writes most of her songs herself and the texts are quite 'deep' if you think about it...
