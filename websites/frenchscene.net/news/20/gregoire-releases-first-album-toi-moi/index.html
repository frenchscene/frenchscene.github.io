Grégoire releases first album 'Toi+Moi'


The first album of French artist Grégoire 'Toi+Moi' is the second best selling album this week. Grégoire was actually discovered by MySpace users. He's the first artist to be signed by 'MyMajorCompany', which makes it possible for internet users to finance an album.
